The Benefits of Yoga

Most people have heard of yoga, but they may not realize the full scope of benefits a consistant yoga practice can provide.

More Than Just Physical Benefits

A consistant yoga practice, as highlighted by John Hopkins Medical, includes many overall health benefits that include:

  • Improved strength, balance, and flexibility
  • Eases arthritis symptoms
  • Reduce levels of stress which can contribute to better heart-health
  • Yoga relaxes and helps with better sleep
  • Yoga helps with managing stress
  • Yoga provides a supportive community

How Yoga Has Evolved into Modern Times

Today, yoga has become a worldwide phenomenon, practiced by millions for its physical, mental, and spiritual benefits. Yoga studios, teacher training programs, and online platforms have made it more accessible. Over time, many different styles of yoga have emerged, blending traditional practices with modern interpretations, including:

  • Vinyasa which is a flowing sequence of movements
  • Power Yoga is a fitness-based style
  • Hot Yoga is practiced in heated rooms
  • Restorative Yoga is focused on relaxation
  • Yin Yoga is slow-paced with a focus on deep stretching
